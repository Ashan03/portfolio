<ProjectSection title="Interaction Framework" id="interaction-model">
    <p>The interaction model tries to prioritize presence over precision.</p>

    <h3>ðŸ‘€  Intent-Based Scoping</h3>
    <p>Wandering without a goal can feel aimless, but wandering with a specific destination feels like a chore. The system bridges this by filtering the map through four emotional states:</p>
    <div class="attribute-pills" style="margin-top: 1rem;">
        <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #38BB39; color: #fff;">Relax</span>
        <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #EA5D49; color: #fff;">Play</span>
        <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #F6F727; color: #1a1a1a;">Social</span>
        <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #F64CB9; color: #fff;">Essentials</span>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top: 1rem;">This allows the backend to generate relevant waypoints while the user feels they are simply choosing a "vibe" rather than a coordinate.</p>

    <p class="centered-tagline">Structure enables spontaneity by narrowing the paradox of choice.</p>

    <FullWidthImage src="/assets/projects/detur/artifact.png" height="400px" caption="Interaction Artifact" constrain={true} />

    <h3>ðŸ§­  Heads-Up Navigation</h3>
    <p>Standard turn-by-turn directions optimize for certainty but demand constant screen attention. This "loose" navigation provides just enough information to keep the user oriented, shifting their primary focus from the screen back to the physical world.</p>

    <p class="centered-tagline">Reducing the interaction cost of wayfinding to keep users present.</p>

    <h3>ðŸŽ°  Gamified Observation</h3>
    <p>To break the habit of "tunnel vision" during commutes, the system interjects with Questsâ€”prompts to find specific textures, colors, or objects (e.g., "Find bird poop," "Capture three doors"). These act as cognitive speed bumps, forcing the user to slow down and actively scan their environment to earn Daily XP.</p>

    <p class="centered-tagline">Validating the detour by turning observation into a quantifiable achievement.</p>
</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Campus Audit" id="study">
    <p>
        Before designing interventions, the study established a baseline by analyzing the campus as a system of interactions. The goal was to identify specific spatial levers that could directly impact the organization’s most critical KPI: creating a stable, non-migratory labor force.
    </p>
    
    <div style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 1rem; margin-top: 1rem;">
        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #F50102; color: #ffffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 700;">Campus Character</span>
            <p>Studying the campus DNA using a structural framework: <span style="font-weight: 500;">Campus + Axes + Massing</span>. The site is organized around rigid "Entry" and "Main" spines. These spines serve as legacy 'Growth' infrastructure—optimized for material throughput. This structural rigidity now bottlenecks the 'Place' phase, isolating the human capital essential for long-term stability.</p>
        </div>

        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #F534A7; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 700;">Campus Edge</span>
            <p>Evaluating the campus perimeter using the framework: <span style="font-weight: 500;">Campus + Town + Connect</span>. This interface currently serves as the critical junction where the industrial ecosystem interacts with the residential town, functioning as a shared space for community engagement. The layout facilitates inter-town collaboration, effectively utilizing the edge to foster social cohesion rather than separation. Research highlights the value of this integration, noting that active campus edges correlate with a 23% increase in community well-being, strengthening the bond between the organization and its locality.</p>
        </div>

        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #F66F06; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 700;">Public Realm</span>
            <p>We audited the shared environments utilizing the framework: <span style="font-weight: 500;">Campus + Public + Community</span>. These spaces currently function as the site's "connective tissue," facilitating movement and linkage between distinct operational zones. The analysis identifies the potential to elevate this network from a functional transit system to a driver of psychological well-being through accessible green infrastructure. Research supports this strategic enhancement, linking environmental quality to a 45% decrease in stress-related absenteeism.</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <PdfEmbed src="/assets/projects/tirupati/Study.pdf" fullWidth={false} />

    <div style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 1rem; margin-top: 1rem;">
        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #3089FE; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 700;">Third Places</span>
            <p>The distribution of informal zones were studied using the framework: <span style="font-weight: 500;">Greens + Access</span>. These areas currently function as decentralized pockets of respite, offering the workforce essential opportunities for decompression and connection to nature. The strategic opportunity lies in programming these spaces to serve as active "Third Places," physically manifesting the "Amara Raja Way" of Entrepreneurship by enabling the spontaneous cross-pollination of ideas. Data supports this activation, showing that effective placemaking drives a 13-16% improvement in peer collaboration and a 19-23% increase in job satisfaction.</p>
        </div>

        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #2FA000; color: #ffffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 700;">System of Parts</span>
            <p>Finally we analysed the macro-level integration of <span style="font-weight: 500;">Production, Innovation, Housing, Services, and Public Spaces</span> to assess the campus's operational unity. The study highlights that these functions achieve maximum impact only when aligned strategies and shared resources bridge the gap between sectors. Transforming these isolated verticals into a cohesive ecosystem is critical for growth; it fosters the cross-pollination of ideas required for innovation while providing the holistic amenities that secure long-term employee retention.</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <hr class="feature-divider" />

    <h3>Scoping the Intervention</h3>
    <p>
        This diagnostic phase successfully mapped the business problems (attrition, isolation, stress) to spatial root causes. This allowed us to scope the design work into four targeted interventions: Landscape, Indoor Experience, Signage & Branding, and Architectural Language—ensuring every design dollar spent addressed a specific systemic gap.
    </p>
</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Systemic Acupuncture" id="implementation">
    <p>
        To translate the "Home Base" vision into reality, the implementation strategy moved beyond a cosmetic renovation to a programmable interface update. We treated the campus as hardware needing a new operating system, defining four strategic pillars
    </p>

    <p class="centered-tagline">Landscape, Indoor Experience, Signage, and Architectural Language</p>

    <p>
        to drive behavior change. This "Systemic Acupuncture" approach targeted high-leverage nodes where minimal physical interventions could yield maximum cultural impact, ensuring that every modification reinforced the "Amara Raja Way."
    </p>

    <PdfEmbed src="/assets/projects/tirupati/Implementation.pdf" fullWidth={false} />

    <div style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 1rem; margin-top: 2rem;">
        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #2d2d2dff; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 500;">Landscape</span>
            <p>We redefined the campus ground plane using the framework: <span style="font-weight: 500;">Streetscape + Vegetation + Nodes</span>. Moving beyond simple beautification, the landscape strategy integrates functionality with aesthetics to shift the user experience from "traversing" to "inhabiting." By activating the "in-between" spaces, we inspire a sense of pride and purpose, turning logistical routes into restorative journeys.</p>
        </div>

        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #2d2d2dff; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 500;">Indoor Experience</span>
            <p>Enhancing the campus required penetrating the "box" to cultivate indoor spaces that foster the "Amara Raja Way." The strategy focuses on <span style="font-weight: 500;">Entry Experience + Breakout Spaces + Air Quality</span>, transforming sterile production environments into zones of creativity and collaboration. This shift ensures that the physical environment actively supports the mental well-being and social connection of the workforce.</p>
        </div>

        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #2d2d2dff; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 500;">Signage & Branding</span>

By weaving identity and purpose amidst the machinery through <span style="font-weight: 500;">Wayfinding + Brand Identity</span>, the signage system elevates the landscape from a functional grid to a narrative experience. This layer guides progress with pride, reinforcing the collective vision at every decision point.</p>
        </div>

        <hr class="feature-divider" style="margin: 0;" />

        <div>
            <span class="attribute-pill" style="background: #2d2d2dff; color: #ffffff; margin-bottom: 0.75rem; font-weight: 500;">Architectural Language</span>

Like threads of a finely woven fabric, aesthetic uniformity stitches together the industrial campus, creating a visual narrative that reflects the organization's strength and resilience. This consistency transforms a collection of buildings into a singular, cohesive institution.</p>
        </div>
